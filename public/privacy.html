So Full!

Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 11, 2026

배불러! (So Full!) is a personal, client-side web app that helps you track and rate the foods and drinks you have tried, including ramyeon, snacks, drinks, and ice cream. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

  • Google account information when you sign in (such as your name, email, and profile photo).
  • The food and drink entries you create in the app, including name, brand, rating, notes, and optional image URL or image Drive metadata.
  • Image files you choose to upload from your device so the app can save them to your Google Drive.
  • Local session data stored in your browser (such as an access token and session timestamps).
  • Sign-in metadata used for security notification emails (sign-in time, device name/model when available, device/browser, and approximate city/country derived from request metadata).

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you with Google and enable access to your data.
  • To save, load, and display your entries and images.
  • To keep your session active in your browser.
  • To send signup and login notification emails.

Where Your Data Is Stored

Entry data is stored in your Google Drive AppData folder using the drive.appdata scope. Uploaded image files are stored in your Google Drive in the 배불러! (So Full!)/images folder using the drive.file scope.

To prevent duplicate emails, the email service stores limited notification state (email address, display name, and last-sent timestamps) in a Firebase Firestore collection.

Service Providers

We use third-party services to operate the app:

  • Google (Firebase Authentication, Google Drive, Google APIs).
  • Brevo for email delivery.
  • GitHub Pages for static site hosting.
  • Vercel for serverless API hosting.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share data with service providers needed to operate the app as described above.

Your Choices

  • You can delete entries in the app. When you delete an entry, it is removed from your Google Drive AppData (and any Drive-stored image for that entry is deleted with it). Once deleted, we do not keep any deleted entry data.
  • You can sign out to remove stored access tokens from your browser.

Retention

Your entries and images remain in your Google Drive until you delete them. Email notification state is retained to prevent duplicate emails; you may request deletion by contacting support.

Security

We use Google Sign-In and OAuth access to interact with your Google Drive. No passwords are stored by the app. All communication uses HTTPS.

Children's Privacy

배불러! (So Full!) is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect the latest version.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@sofull.site.
